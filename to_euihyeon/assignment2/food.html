kimchi
fermented food
1 1 hour Senior level 248
Kimchi is a preserved fermented food in Korea made by pickling cabbage and radish with coarse salt, washing it, and mixing seasonings such as red pepper powder, green onions, garlic, ginger, and salted fish. It is both a traditional Korean food and a representative food.
Ingredients: Radish, red pepper powder, minced garlic, green onion, salted salt
